Membership Benefits

We call forward our audience to be a part of our team and fill in the below registration form to become a member of our orchestra family. This also comes up with various benefits such as:

  • The members get an additional pass on purchase of every single pass for entries to our concerts.
  • They may also book the orchestra for their parties at 40% off.
  • The members get to win amazing prices in our annual lucky-draws.
  • Barrier Reef Orchestra also recognizes the achievements of its members and appreciates them in front of its family. Members are awarded and honored for their excellent contributions to the society.
